Biography

I'm a second-year Master student in Department of AeroSpace Information Engineering, Beihang University, advised by Prof. Xiaoyan Luo. Previously, I received the B.S. in School of Astronautics from Beihang University. I was a research intern at 3D Vision and Computational Photography Group in Tsinghua University from Sept.2022 to Jun.2024, under the guidance of Prof. Yebin Liu.
